Dinner is a potluck. Bring your fave finger food, a scrumptious salad or side, or a decadent dessert in a serving dish with a utensil. We'll supply beef and veggie burgers (made with love by Karilyn and Simon).

Free shuttles from Reveslstoke will be running at 14:00, 15:00 and 15:30 to bring you out to the venue. Shuttles will return to Revelstoke at 23:00, midnight and 1:00.

Feel like partying all night for Wexfest? Bring a tent and stay right on the property with us. There's lots of flat space for setting up camp. Once you commit to skipping the shuttle, you're there for the night, so bring earplugs if you're a light sleeper. Really though, you're better off keeping on dancing!

Note: there are no pets allowed at the wedding venue.

Accommodation

Looking for a place to stay in Revelstoke? There are many options available, though please book sooner rather than later to ensure you get a place. It's a long weekend with lots of fun events happening in town, including a baseball tournament, a mountain bike race, and the third annual Revelstoke Bike Fest.

We've reserved blocks of rooms at a few hotels. Please reference KEMPTON/WEX when booking.

Monashee Lodge: ($99 + tax). Call 1-250-814-2553 to book. IMPORTANT: Please book before June 5 at the Monashee Lodge. Monashee Lodge offers several different room types if you're travelling with family and friends, all at the same rate, so bunk up!

Powder Springs Motel: ($89 + tax). Call 1-800-991-4455 to book. This is pretty standard motel located right downtown, rooms range from so-so to pretty good.

We've also got private house and/or apartment accommodation options through a local property management company. Email us for more information on how to book those.

There are also camping options available:

  • Lamplighter Campground
  • KOA Campground
  • Williamson Lake Campground

You're also welcome to camp right at the wedding venue on Friday and Saturday nights. In fact, we'd love for you to stay out there with us the night of the wedding - that's where we'll be camping!

Flying in from out of town and want to camp out the night of the wedding? Let us know in advance and we'll start collecting gear for those who need. There's washrooms, a kitchen and running water, so don't worry about it being too rustic. We just want to give folks the option of dancing all night and not worrying about transportation back into Revelstoke.
