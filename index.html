The Likely Pair

Our epic eight-hour first date started at a Starbucks, an unlikely location considering how seriously Simon takes coffee (I had to read The Professional Barista's Handbook before using our espresso machine). Since then, we've shared countless lattes, laughs and late night dinners, and we get a lifetime more.

Simon has a winning combination of skill, charm, brilliance and plain ol' good luck - it makes him a great partner in crime! He's got an unwavering desire to learn, create and build, and he loves to teach and encourage others. If he doesn't have the answer he can quickly figure it out, and he willingly lends a hand to anyone who needs it. He's taught me the importance of not being afraid to fail, and he's always been my loudest cheerleader. Thanks, buddy!

Things always turn out great - it's called the Wex Effect.

- Karilyn

On our first date, I looked across the table to see Karilyn finding her way to the bottom of my favourite Vancouver meal. A tsunami of phở had already enveloped the teapot and when she looked up she had the look of a puppy caught chewing on a fine pair of Italian shoes. Instead of shoelaces, sprouts and vermicelli.

Karilyn's unabashed ability to enjoy the moment and project sunshine has helped me grow into the man I am today. I'm excited to share the rest of my life with my best friend and share that promise with you all in August.

Karilyn's the bomb and that's why I choo-choo-choose her.

- Simon

Bridesmaids

  • Andrea "Bees" Keesey

    Karilyn and Andrea met during their English masters degrees at Simon Fraser University. They provided an invaluable support system to each other bolstered by plenty of wine. Together they made it through grad school and became life-long best buds. They tried downhill mountain biking for the first time together in Whistler and surprisingly no one needed stitches.

    Hailing from Saskatchewan, Andrea lives in Vancouver with her husband Ben - they got married May 3 in Hawaii (congrats!). We were friends well before we knew Ben or Simon!

  • Lindsay "Bear Claw" Davis

    Bear Claw is a BC legend, scoring a BC Outdoors cover photo holding a salmon nearly her size that was taken during one of her four years at a remote BC sportfishing lodge. She'll out-fish you, beat you on the golf course, get your truck un-stuck for you, and then drink you under the table. Don't be fooled by the diminutive stature - Bear Claw charges hard.

    She and Karilyn met a few years ago and have been pretty much inseperable ever since as biking, skiing and wine-drinking buddies. She's longtime roommates with Kitty, also a bridesmaid. The ladies spend a LOT of girl time together!

  • Sarah "Sarahsaurus" Wex

    Simon's younger sister Sarah was the first Wex to be born in Canada (but assembled in South Africa). She can make Simon laugh harder than anyone, and does it often. Sarah spent a fun year living in Revelstoke several seasons back and we all had a blast spending time skiing together.

    Sarah lives in Vancouver. An avid cyclist, she recently purchased her first car: Peppercorn L. Wexbottom. Congrats on the new addition to the family!

  • Anna "Banana" Wex

    Anna is a world traveler who has lived in Vancouver, Barcelona, Cologne, London, ON, and Vancouver - and that's just in the last five years! Simon's older sister, a part of Anna's heart lives in South Africa where she spent a lot of time as a kid.

    Anna is an architect with a great eye for style, design and organization, making her super helpful for throwing a wedding (and for life in general) - she's more organized about the wedding than we are! She and her partner Mike Martin live in the Gastown area of Vancouver and have a view of the North Shore that can make a grown man weep.

  • Kat "Kitty" Cadegan

    Many ladies at the wedding will no doubt be wearing amazing jewelry made by Kat, a Revelstoke silversmith (check out some of her wares on Etsy!). Kat and her Mexican pooch Señor are the cutest couple in town. Kat will be coming off a summer as a treeplanting foreman, so she'll likely be the most tanned, toned and ready to party.

    Kitty is an exceptional baker, an incredibly generous friend, and a dose of welcome comic relief in all our lives. Revelstoke is lucky to have her, and we're lucky to have her as a bridesmaid!

  • Sarah "Red" Peterson

    From close friend to closer next-door-neighbour, Red eats as many meals at Simon and KK's house as at her own, and comes over for many coffees a week. If she's not at theirs they are at hers - there is talk of digging a tunnel, or at least stringing up tin can telephones.

    Red helps all her friends stay happy, healthy and well-fed, and her enthusiasm is infectious. Red has been in Revelstoke for six years and is kind of a big deal. An party-planning extraordinaire, she organizes Revesltoke's best events and good times!
