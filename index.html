Bridesmaids

  • Andrea "Bees" Keesey

    Karilyn and Andrea met during their English masters degrees at Simon Fraser University, where they provided an invaluable support system to each other bolstered by plenty of wine. They tried downhill mountain biking for the first time together in Whistler and no one needed stitches.

    Hailing from Saskatchewan, Andrea lives in Vancouver with her fiancé Ben, who she marries on May 3 in Hawaii.

  • Lindsay "Bear Claw" Davis

    Bear Claw is a BC legend, scoring a BC Outdoors cover photo holding a salmon almost the size of her, taken during one of her four years at a remote BC sportfishing lodge. She'll out-fish you, beat you on the golf course, get your truck un-stuck for you, and then drink you under the table.

    Lindsay and Karilyn are Revelstoke biking, skiing and wine-drinking buddies.

  • Simon's younger sister Sarah was the first Wex to be born in Canada. She can make Simon laugh harder than anyone, and does it often. Sarah spent a fun year living in Revelstoke several seasons back and we all had a blast spending time skiing together.

    Sarah lives in Vancouver. An avid cyclist, she recently purchased her first car: Peppercorn L. Wexbottom.

  • Anna "Banana" Wex

    Anna is a world traveler who has lived in Vancouver, Barcelona, Cologne, London, ON, and Vancouver in the last five years, and many more beyond that. Anna is an architect with a great eye for style, design and organization, making her super helpful to throwing a wedding!

  • Kat "Kitty" Cadegan

    There's no doubt that many ladies at the wedding will be wearing amazing jewelry made by Kat, a Revelstoke silversmith. Kat and her Mexican dog Señor are the cutest couple in town. Kat will be coming off a summer as a treeplanting foreman, so no doubt she'll be the most tanned and toned. Kat is an exceptional baker, an incredibly generous friend, and often our comic relief.

  • Sarah "Red" Peterson

    From close friend to closer next-door-neighbour, Red eats as many meals at Simon and KK's house as at her own, and is there for many coffees a week. If she's not at theirs they are at hers - there is talk of digging a tunnel.

    Red has been in Revelstoke for six years and is kind of a big deal. An party-planning extraordinaire, she organizes Revesltoke's best events and good times!
