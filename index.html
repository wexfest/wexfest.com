Accommodation

Looking for a place to stay in Revelstoke? There are many options available, though please book soon to ensure you get a place. It's a long weekend with lots of fun events happening in town, including a baseball tournament, a mountain bike race, and the third annual Revelstoke Bike Fest.

We've reserved blocks of rooms at a few hotels. Please reference KEMPTON/WEX when booking.

Monashee Lodge: ($99 + tax). Call 1-250-814-2553 to book. IMPORTANT: Please book before mid June at the Monashee Lodge! Monashee Lodge offers several different room types all for the same rate if you're travelling with family and/or friends, so bunk up!

Powder Springs Motel: ($89 + tax). Call 1-800-991-4455 to book. This is pretty standard motel located right downtown.

We've also got private house and/or apartment accommodation options through a local property management company starting at $200/night and sleeping a bunch. Email us for more information on how to book those.

There are also camping options available:

  • Lamplighter Campground
  • KOA Campground
  • Williamson Lake Campground

You're also welcome to camp right at the wedding venue on Friday and Saturday nights. In fact, we'd love for you to stay out there with us the night of the wedding - that's where we'll be camping!

Flying in from out of town and want to camp out the night of the wedding? Let us know in advance and we'll start collecting gear for those who need. There's washrooms, a kitchen and running water, so don't worry about it being too rustic. We just want to give folks the option of dancing all night and not worrying about transportation back into Revelstoke.

For any questions please email: us@wexfest.com
